About me

Jungang Yang is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in computer science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University(SJTU), under the supervision of Prof. Liyao Xiang. You can find more information about Prof. Xiang here. specializing in differential privacy, Adversarial learning and Federated Learning.

He received the Bachelor's degree in Information and Computing Science from Nanjing University, Nanjing, China, in 2019.

Research

Current research interests focuses on:

  • Differential privacy (差分隐私)
  • Adversarial learning(强化学习)
  • Federated Learning(联邦学习)
