About me

Jungang Yang is currently an assistant professor at Shanghai University(SHU).

He obtained his Ph.D. Degree in Computer Science and Technology from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2024 under the supervision of Prof. Liyao Xiang. His research focuses on differential privacy, adversarial learning, and federated learning. He received the Bachelor's degree in Information and Computing Science from Nanjing University, Nanjing, China, in 2019.

Undergraduate students interested in Differential Privacy or Unmanned Gaming Confrontation research are very welcome to contact with e-mail or Wechat!

Research

Current research interests focuses on:

  • Differential privacy (差分隐私)
  • Adversarial learning(对抗学习)
  • Federated Learning(联邦学习)
