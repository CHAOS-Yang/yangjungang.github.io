About me

Jungang Yang is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in computer science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), under the supervision of Prof. Liyao Xiang. You can find more information about Prof. Xiang here. His research focuses on differential privacy, adversarial learning, and federated learning.

He received his Bachelor's degree in Information and Computing Science from Nanjing University, China, in 2019.

Research

Current research interests focus on:

  • Differential privacy (差分隐私)
  • Adversarial learning (强化学习)
  • Federated Learning (联邦学习)
